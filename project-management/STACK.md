# Stack decisions

Permanent decisions that survive any single session. Record here when you make a choice that shouldn't be revisited without strong reason — a dependency pin, an architectural pattern, a rejected alternative.

Read the governing philosophy section on the first session of each day. Read in full before any architecture or permanent decision.

---

## Governing philosophy

- **Decisions that are made don't get remade.** If it's on this list, it was decided. Reopen only with new evidence, not preference.
- **Record why alternatives were ruled out.** The reason matters more than the choice — it's what lets you judge edge cases later.
- **Permanent decisions are rare.** Most choices belong in the pre-build lock or the log. Only record something here if it constrains future builds.
